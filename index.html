§ 08 How we work

A first conversation, candid and free. Four stages — from listening to evaluating what changed.

  1. I

    Listening

    A short, honest first conversation. What is the question behind the question? Is HDC the right fit — and if not, who is? No obligation, no script, no fee.

    ◇ ≈30 minutes · No obligation, no fee

  2. II

    Proposal

    A written scope mapped to your organisation: aims, audience, design assumptions, milestones, fee. Plain language. Costs clearly broken out. Revisable.

    ◇ 1–2 weeks · Written, revisable

  3. III

    Delivery

    Designed and led by Helen. Bespoke materials. ILM-mapped where accredited credit is wanted. Delivered in person in London and nationally, or online — typically blended.

    ◇ 3–12 months · Blended, in-person and online

  4. IV

    Evaluation

    ISO 9000 evaluation on every engagement — participant outcomes, sponsor reflection, and what the institution does next. We close the loop on what worked, what didn't, and what the work surfaced.

    ◇ Continuous · Closes the loop
