Service I · Consultancy & Interim Management
Eight areas of expertise · Education, public & private sectors

Management & education consultancy.

Expert consultancy that drives performance, cultural change, and inclusive leadership across the education, public and private sectors — delivered as an embedded engagement or as interim management when leadership capacity is short.

HDC works with senior teams on the questions that don't have neat answers — strategy under constraint, organisational change, inclusive leadership, the quality and design of learning. Eight named areas of expertise; each delivered alone or in combination, scoped to your organisation and your timetable.

  1. 01

    AI & Learning Technology Consultancy

    Specialist support to confidently navigate AI and digital innovation in management practices, and in learning and development. Strategy, policy, staff capability, and the equity questions that matter before the technology decides them for you.

  2. 02

    Strategic & Operational Management

    Strengthens leadership, improves systems, and aligns teams for measurable impact. Diagnostic, recommendation, and delivery support across complex organisations.

  3. 03

    Equality, Diversity & Inclusion

    Embeds inclusive practices into leadership, policies, and team development. See the full EDI consultancy and training catalogue →

  4. 04

    Policy & Strategy Development

    Co-creates evidence-based strategies aligned with organisational values and statutory obligations. Plain language, clear ownership, realistic timeframes.

  5. 05

    Continuing Professional Development (CPD)

    Tailored programmes that build capacity and develop inclusive practice in real teams, not the abstract. Mapped to your organisation, accredited where wanted.

  6. 06

    Curriculum & Programme Design

    Supports education providers in designing inclusive, responsive learning experiences across levels and modes — face-to-face, online, blended.

  7. 07

    Transformation & Change Management

    Helps organisations move through significant change with clarity and engagement — sequencing, communication, and the structures staff need to come with you.

  8. 08

    Quality Improvement & Delivery

    Identifies operational gaps and delivers practical solutions that lift performance, learner outcomes, and staff satisfaction. ISO 9000 evaluated on every engagement.
