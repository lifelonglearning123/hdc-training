Service II · Equality, Diversity & Inclusion
13 consultancy services · 14 named courses

Inclusion that moves beyond compliance.

EDI consultancy and training services delivered in partnership with client organisations. Assignments range from facilitating individual workshops to more complex strategic and operational consultancy projects.

EDI work at HDC is built on three commitments. Equality — fairness, and ensuring individuals aren't treated less favourably because of protected characteristics. Diversity — recognising, respecting and celebrating each other's differences. Inclusion — creating environments where people feel valued, with awareness of unconscious biases and the strategies to manage them.
