Service III · Training & Development
Over 20 years · ILM & City & Guilds accredited

Training and development, delivered properly.

HDC has delivered courses for over twenty years — face-to-face and online. Our virtual learning environment is inclusive, personal, friendly, and built around individual needs. Three delivery channels; six course categories.

Training at HDC is bespoke by default. Off-the-shelf courses are available — but the work most clients ask us to do is built for them: mapped to their organisation, accredited where wanted, sequenced around their delivery rhythm.

A

HDC Training Centre

Our primary training facility — equipped for face-to-face delivery and accredited course administration. ILM and City & Guilds approved centre (No. 067465).

B

Courses & Workshops

Six course categories — accredited and bespoke. Each runs as a public open course or as a tailored in-house programme.

  1. 01

    Institute of Leadership & Management (ILM) Accredited Courses

    Recognised leadership qualifications, mapped to ILM Levels and delivered with an assessed change project running through the programme.

  2. 02

    Leading & Managing

    Practical leadership and management development for first-line, middle and senior managers — across single workshops and longer programmes.

  3. 03

    Coaching Courses

    Coaching skills for leaders and managers, drawing on Helen's Postgraduate Diploma in Coaching and twenty years of practice.

  4. 04

    Improving Training, Teaching & Learning

    CPD for teachers, trainers and assessors — pedagogy, curriculum design, inclusive practice, and quality improvement.

  5. 05

    Business Enterprise & Development

    Six courses in business and enterprise — practical skills for staff and learners moving into commercial or entrepreneurial work.

  6. 06

    Personal Development

    Self-awareness, communication, confidence, resilience — the skills that hold leadership and inclusive practice together.

C

HDC Virtual Learning Environment

Online delivery platform for remote training access. The VLE is inclusive, personal, friendly, and based entirely on your individual needs — designed for cohorts who can't always meet in person, or for organisations that need a permanent learning home.

For accredited learners and corporate cohorts, the VLE hosts course materials, recorded sessions, assessments, and the action-learning sets that run between live sessions.
